LET'S TALK ABOUT SECURITY.

Malicious Websites

QR codes can lead to fake websites designed to steal your personal information.

Automatic Downloads

Some QR codes trigger automatic downloads of malware or viruses.

Payment Scams

Fraudsters can replace legitimate payment QR codes with their own to steal money.

Stay Safe

Always verify the source of QR codes and use a scanner app that previews URLs before visiting them.
